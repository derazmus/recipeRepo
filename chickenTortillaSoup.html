Chicken Tortilla Soup

Ingrediants

  • 1 yellow onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 3/4 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast
  • Fajita Seasoning Packet
  • Fresh Black Pepper
  • 1 15 ounce can fire roasted tomatoes
  • 1 10 ounce can diced tomatoes with green chiles
  • 1 15 ounce can black beans
  • 1 cup frozen corn kernals
  • 6 cups chicken broth

    • Instructions

    1. Dice the onion and mince the garlic. Add the onion, garlic, chicken breast, fajita seasoning packet, some fresh cracker pepper (about 10 crackes of a pepper mill), the fire roasted tomatoes (with juices), diced tomatoes with chiles(with juices), black beans(drained), corn and chicken broth in the slow cooker.

    2. Give everything a brief stir, place the lid on the slow cooker, and cook on high for 4 hours, or low for 8 hours.

    3. After cooking on high for 4 hours or low for 8 hours, carefully remove the chicken breast, shred it with a fork, and then return to the soup.

    4. To serve, ladle the soup into bowls, add topings if desired (i.e. tortilla chips, cilatro, avocado or a splash of lime juice).
